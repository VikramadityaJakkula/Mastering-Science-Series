THEORY OF ADDITION

What is addition? Addition is the math function that lets you know how much you have when you combine two or more numbers. Every time you put money into your bank account, you are adding to your balance. At the grocery store, you add items to your cart.

Addition involves putting two or more quantities to find the sum. Some basic funda :

  • Numbers to be added are called addends.
  • The result of addition of numbers is called sum.

 Example :     36  + 25 = 61.      Here  , 36 and 25 are the addends. Sum = 61.

STEPS INVOLVED

We will discuss the method of finding the sum of two numbers by adding their place values.

Lets take up an example :    

                        37
                     + 10 

ADDING AFTER EXPANSION
Expand the Numbers:
37 = 3 Tens + 7 Ones = 30 + 7
10 = 1 Tens + 0 Ones = 10 + 0

Step 1: Add the Ones place value 
7 + 0 = 7 Onesre 7 is taken into the sum.
OnesOnes place value : 7

Step 2: Add the Tens place value 
3 + 1 = 4 Tensre 4 is taken into the sum.
TensTens place value : 4

Answer : 4 Tens + 7 Ones = 40 + 7 = 47 

Similarly for larger numbers we continue adding the values from the right to 
the left.i.e from the ONES place to the higher places.
Now , we will try to simplify the process of performing the addition by stacking the numbers in their
 place values.

ADDING BY STACK
To stack numbers::
    - Place the numbers you want to add on top of each other in their value places.
    - Place the plus sign,+, on the left of the stack
    - Draw a line at the bottom.
    
                                         37
                                      + 10
                                      
Add the digits in the ONES place :      
                                         37
                                      + 10
                                           7
Then add the digits in the TENS place : 
                                         37
                                      + 10
                                         47
                                        
If the addends have digits in higher place values continue adding.
DEALING WITH CARRY
If you want to add 42 and 49, the steps are a little different because you'll need to carry a number to the next place value. You carry when the numbers in a place value add up to more than 9. This is an important skill you'll need to learn in order to do some addition. So here lets examine how we deal with this situation.

                                        42
                                    +  49
ADDING BY EXPANSION
Expand the Numbers:
42 = 4 Tens + 2 Ones = 40 + 2
49 = 4 Tens + 9 Ones = 40 + 9

Step 1: Add the Ones place value 
2 + 9 = 11 Ones
11 Ones = 1 Tens + 1 Ones
Here 1 is taken into the sum and 1 is carried to the Tens place level.
Ones place value : 1

Step 2: Add the Tens place value along with the carry.
4 + 4 + 1 = 9 Tens
Here 9 is taken into the sum.
Tens place value : 9

Answer : 9 Tens + 1 Ones = 90 + 1 = 91 

Now lets perform the same operation by the stack method. 

ADDING BY STACK
Add the ONES place value :
                            Carry:   1
                                          42
                                       + 49
                                            1
                                        
Now add the TENS place value along with the carry.
                            Carry:   1
                                          42
                                       + 49
                                          91
