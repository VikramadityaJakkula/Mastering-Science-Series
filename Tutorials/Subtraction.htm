INTRODUCTION
Definition: Subtraction means reducing or taking away a number from another to find the difference. We subtract the subtrahend from the minuend to get the difference.So if we say 96 – 32= 64, remember to call them by their proper terminology 
Minuend   Subtrahend   Difference
96 – 32 = 64
 Based on the subtraction facts we can derive an important property that the sum of subtrahend and difference is always equal to Minuend. It’s a good way to verify your answer. 
Subtrahend   Difference   Minuend
64 + 32 = 96
  
STEPS INVOLVED

We will discuss the method of finding the  difference of two numbers.

Lets take up an example :    

                       25
                     - 11 

SUBTRACTING THROUGH EXPANSION
Expand the Numbers :
25 = 2 Tens + 5 Ones = 20 + 5
11 = 1 Tens + 1 Ones = 10 + 1

Step 1: Subtract the Ones place value.
Then 5 - 1 = 4 Ones
Ones place value: 4

Step 2: Subtract the Tens place value.
Then 2 - 1 = 1 Tens
Tens place value: 1

Answer : 1 Tens + 4 Ones = 10 + 4 = 14
Similarly for larger numbers we continue subtracting the values from the right to
the left.i.e from the ONES place to the higher places.
Now , we will try to simplify the process of performing the subtraction by stacking the numbers in their
place values.

SUBTRACTING BY STACK
To stack numbers::
    - Place the numbers you want to add on top of each other in their value places.
    - Place the plus sign, -, on the left of the stack
    - Draw a line at the bottom.
   
                            25
                          - 11
                                      
Subtract the digits in the ONES place :     

                                                                      25
                          - 11
                             4
Then subtract the digits in the TENS place :
                            25
                          - 11
                            14
                                        
If the there are digits in higher place values continue subtracting.
